About Ecomen Mining Pvt. Ltd

Pioneering Mining & Environmental Expertise

Ecomen is a dynamic, multidisciplinary consulting firm specializing in mineral exploration, mine operations, and environmental impact assessments. We deliver science-driven solutions that help businesses effectively navigate environmental, social, and economic challenges.

  • Approved by the Ministry of Environment & Forests, Government of India
  • Designated as an Environmental Laboratory under the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986
  • 500+ ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESSMENTS
  • 14+ NABET Accredited Sectors

Mision

To provide expert advice, guidance, and solutions to clients in order to help them achieve their goals, overcome challenges, and maximize their potential.

Vision

To become a global leader in sustainable resource management, environmental stewardship, and innovative mining practices.

Values

Sustainability, Scientific Excellence, Integrity & Compliance, Collaboration, Innovation
