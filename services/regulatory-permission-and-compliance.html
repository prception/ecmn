Regulatory Permissions & Compliance

ECOMEN provides expert assistance in obtaining various regulatory permissions required for mining operations, ensuring compliance with environmental and industrial regulations.

Services We Provide

106(2)(b) Permission for Mining Operations

  • Guidance in obtaining permission under Rule 106(2)(b) of the Metalliferous Mines Regulations (MMR), 1961.
  • Preparation of mine safety and operational plans.
  • Submission of documents to the Directorate General of Mines Safety (DGMS).
  • Compliance with safety and hazard management regulations.

Consent to Establish (CTE) & Consent to Operate (CFO)

  • Assistance in obtaining Consent to Establish (CTE) and Consent to Operate (CFO) from the State Pollution Control Board (SPCB) under the Air & Water Acts.
  • Preparation and submission of required documentation, including environmental management plans.
  • Liaison with authorities to expedite approvals.
  • Ensuring compliance with pollution control norms.

IBM Registration & Mining Licenses

  • Assistance in registration with the Indian Bureau of Mines (IBM) for statutory compliance.
  • Filing of returns as per Mineral Conservation & Development Rules (MCDR), 2017.
  • Procurement of mining leases and prospecting licenses.
  • Assistance in obtaining Mining Plan Approval from IBM.
