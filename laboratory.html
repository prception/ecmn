About Ecomen Laboratories Pvt. Ltd

History

We are an NABL-Certified Laboratory established in the year 1990 by Dr. Binay Prakash Pandey with full-fledged labs at Lucknow, Patna, Hyderabad & Bangalore all of which are NABL accredited & Govt. approved with a Vision to establish a globally benchmarked Food, Water & Environment testing Lab & provide accurate, reliable, consistent results using the most advanced analytical instruments available in the world.

Industry Standards & Certifications

  • NABL Certified
  • USEPA (United States Environmental Protection Agency)
  • BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards)
  • WHO (World Health Organization)
  • APHA (American Public Health Association)

Facilities & Infrastructure

Equipped with some of the most advanced, hi-tech, precise analytical & testing instruments like ICP-MS (Agilent – USA), LCMS-MS (Waters-USA), GCMS-MS, QToF capable of detecting toxins & impurities at ultra-trace, part per trillion levels & spread across 10,000 sqft. with dedicated Microbiology, Wet chemistry, Advanced instrumentation & Sample preparation areas our infrastructure is unique, top class & futuristic.

Scope

Our scope covers purity & impurity profiling, assays, pathogens, endotoxin, heavy metals, minerals, radio actives, drugs of abuse screening, detection of poisonous & toxic elements, presence of steroids & allopathic drugs in ayurvedic & herbal supplements, nutritional analysis, Vitamins, proximates, pesticide residues, antibiotic residues, stability studies, environmental toxins, method development verification & validation, mycotoxins, PAH, PCB, ETO residues, PFAS etc.

Ecomen Laboratory Services

Our Comprehensive Laboratory Testing Services


We provide reliable and certified laboratory testing services to ensure environmental safety, regulatory compliance, and public health. Our advanced testing methodologies deliver accurate results across various industries.

Water Testing Lab

Our water testing lab is a highly sophisticated facility capable of detecting and quantifying more than 1000 parameters at ultra trace levels covering Physical, Chemical, Microbiological, Pesticide residues, Heavy metals, & Organic toxins. click for more details+

Ores, Minerals & Heavy metal Testing Lab

From % to ppt, our facilities & infrastructure can detect & simultaneously quantify 62 elements including heavy metals, minerals, radioactives, transition metals, noble metals & trace impurities. click for more details+

Environment Testing Lab

Covering stack, Ambient air, Noise level, Soil, Effluent, Light Intensity etc., at Ecomen we have one of the most sophisticated & advanced facilities in the world for analysis of contemporary pollutants like PFAS, VOCs, Radioactive etc. click for more details+

Drug Testing Lab

Our highly qualified & experienced technical scientists are adept at method development, validation, assay, purity & impurity profiling involving MS, LC & routine wet chemistry analytical techniques. click for more details+

Food Testing Lab

From nutrition labeling to shelf life studies & adulteration tests; our food testing lab analyzes every aspect of purity & safety covering everything from cooked & processed food to spices, fruits & vegetables, raw materials & ready to eats. click for more details+

Advanced Clinical Testing Lab

Scope covers screening & confirmation for Drugs of Abuse, Therapeutic drug monitoring, Poison detection, Test for presence of steroids & hormones, heavy metal screening in blood, urine, hair etc. click for more details+
